Collage

I created my first digital collage in 2006. After having collected all sorts of things since childhood, the flamboyant wig wearing gentleman was responsible for turning my collections into a creative laboratory. I found him on a leaflet in Barcelona, announcing some major gay disco event. When I came back home, he just fit right in with the glossy collectable pictures I had gotten from my sister as a kid. In the background I put a starry sky and space rockets from my father's old trading cards collection. The resulting piece can be seen underneath. For my traditional, scissors and glue collage artwork, please visit my Behance profile
