Animation

During my stay as an animator at the Fuse School I got the chance to learn and perfect a simplified animation technique using Adobe Premiere and After Effects. Our aim was to produce high quality educational videos as efficiently as possible. I opted for a cartoon inspired style, based on my own pen drawings, which I scanned and optimized for digital use.
